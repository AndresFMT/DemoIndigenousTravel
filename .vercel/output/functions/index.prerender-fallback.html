Who We Are

Indigenous Tourism Manitoba exists to support the Indigenous tourism industry in Manitoba by providing leadership, sharing of stories, and providing the support to strengthen the Indigenous tourism economy in Manitoba. ITM aligns with the mandates of Travel Manitoba and the national Indigenous Tourism Association of Canada (ITAC).
